Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your perfect winter sun getaway with Emirates.

What destinations are included in the winter sun promotion?
Our winter sun destinations include Thailand, Seychelles, Mauritius, Australia, and many more tropical paradises. Each destination offers unique experiences and warm weather perfect for escaping the winter cold. Visit our destinations page to explore all available options.
When is the best time to book my winter sun holiday?
We recommend booking at least 6-8 weeks in advance to secure the best fares and availability. Peak winter months (December to March) tend to book up quickly, so early booking is essential. Our Best Fare Finder can help you find the most competitive prices.
What is included in the Emirates winter sun packages?
Our packages typically include round-trip flights, hotel accommodations, airport transfers, and selected meals depending on the destination. Premium packages may also include additional tours and experiences. Check individual destination pages for specific inclusions.
Can I earn Emirates Skywards Miles on winter sun bookings?
Yes, you can earn Emirates Skywards Miles on all eligible flights and hotel bookings. Miles earning depends on your fare type and membership tier. Premium members enjoy additional benefits and bonus miles. Join Skywards for free to start earning immediately.
What health and safety measures are in place?
Emirates maintains the highest health and safety standards with enhanced cleaning protocols, HEPA air filtration systems, and flexible booking policies. We comply with all destination health requirements and provide up-to-date travel information for all destinations.
How do I check visa requirements for my destination?
Visa requirements vary by destination and nationality. Use our visa checker tool or contact our customer service team for the most current information. We also offer visa assistance services for select destinations to make your travel planning easier.
